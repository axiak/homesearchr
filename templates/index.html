Welcome!

I'm glad you're visiting. I created this site to make my apartment hunt easier, but I hope that this helps you out too.

What is this?

When searching for apartments you have several options: you can search through craigslist, other online media, network, or talk to a broker. The largest advantage of a broker is that they can keep an eye out for you. That's what this web site aims to do—keep an eye out for you. You tell us what to look for, we email you when we find something that looks good.

How does it work?

It's very simple. The site is currently constantly searching Craigslist for the deals that best match your criteria. When it finds something that looks good—and only then—it notifies you.
